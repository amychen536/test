DMV Coffee Shop Business Strategy

1. The LoyalTerp committed to helping chain coffee shops that do not have blockchain technology build their own blockchain and reward tokens to increase sales and brand value.

2. The top-level objective is increasing sales by 20% in the next three years.

3. Since blockchain technology has not been widely used, use differentiation to attract new customers and retain customers by distributing reward tokens that do not expire.

4. Using blockchain technology to attract young customers, who are more willing to try new technologies and will be the main force in future market consumption.

5. One core principle of blockchain technology is irreversibility of records. It prevents insider fraud. And, it is convenient for the owner to view sales records and control internal management.

6. Every purchase is greater than 10 dollars, get 1 token. Every purchase is greater than 30 dollars, and gets 1.1 tokens. Set a promotion day when customers would earn double the amount of token.

7. Earned tokens could exchange coffee products or transfer to other customers.
